About Us

For more than 130 years, PZ Cussons has been creating products to delight, care for and nourish consumers, and we have found a place in millions of households across the world.

Our Dedication

Our dedication to upholding the highest standards and doing the right thing as a company is captured in our purpose:

For everyone, for life, for good.

Following through with this purpose, and backed by our strategy; Building brands for life, today and for future generations, we have created leading brands in three core categories: Hygiene, Baby and Beauty. Premier Cool is a leading brand in the personal care and hygiene category.

Join Us on This Journey

In 2010, we extended the strong equity of Premier into the antibac segment with the launch of our Premier Cool Soap, in three variants. With a brand promise to deliver 25HR freshness and confidence for the modern man.

Premier Cool Deo contains real menthol extracts that give you that icy-cool feel, a refreshing long-lasting and an antibacterial ingredient that helps preserve your freshness all day long.

In 2022, we launched the Premier Cool Back Soap. An embodiment of our ever-evolving brand commitment, to redefine men's grooming and self-care habits. We have established ourselves as a lifestyle choice for the modern who is confident in his masculinity and how he expresses it.
